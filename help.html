Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my order? ▼
Go to the Orders page from the top navigation. Find the order you want to cancel and click the Cancel Order button. You can only cancel orders that are not already completed or failed. Once cancelled, the status will update immediately.
How can I track my order status? ▼
Visit the Orders page to see the real-time status of all your orders. Statuses include: Pending, Completed, Cancelled, and Failed. Each order shows the items, delivery address, and total amount.
Can I reorder a previous order? ▼
Yes! On the Orders page, every order has a Reorder button. Clicking it adds all items from that order back into your cart so you can quickly place the same order again.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We currently support Paytm for online payments. When you confirm your order, a payment confirmation dialog will appear. Simply confirm to complete the payment. More payment options like UPI, cards, and net banking are coming soon.
What happens if my payment fails? ▼
If a payment fails, the order will be marked as Failed in your Orders page. No amount will be deducted. You can try placing the order again from the menu or use the Reorder button on the failed order.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Our average delivery time is under 30 minutes. Delivery time may vary depending on your location, order size, and current demand. You'll receive your food hot and fresh, guaranteed.
Which areas do you deliver to? ▼
We currently deliver within the city limits. Enter your pincode during checkout to confirm if we deliver to your area. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones — check back soon if we don't cover your area yet.
How do I create an account? ▼
Click the Login button on the top right of the menu page. Switch to the Create Account tab and fill in your name, email, phone, address, and password. Your account is saved locally and you'll be logged in immediately.
How do I log out? ▼
On the main menu page, click your name shown in the top right corner. A logout option will appear. Clicking it will sign you out and return you to the login screen.
How do I add or remove items from my cart? ▼
Click on any menu item to open its detail page. Use the quantity selector to choose how many you want, then click Add to Cart. To remove items, open the cart from the menu page and use the remove button next to each item.
